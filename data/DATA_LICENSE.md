# Data rights and reuse notice

The numerical dataset in this repository is a curated reconstruction derived from the supplementary numerical data reported by Qiao et al.:

Qiao, Q., et al. *The use of machine learning to predict the effects of cryoprotective agents on the GelMA-based bioinks used in extrusion cryobioprinting.* Bio-Design and Manufacturing, 2023, 6(4), 464-477.

The repository authors do not claim ownership of the original experimental measurements and do not grant broader rights than they possess. The software license in the repository-level `LICENSE` file applies to the original code and documentation, not automatically to the source-derived experimental values.

Users who reuse or redistribute the data should:

1. cite the original Qiao et al. study;
2. cite the associated secondary-analysis manuscript and repository once available;
3. comply with the terms governing the original publication and supplementary materials;
4. preserve the provenance statement and the record of the four unmeasured formulation conditions; and
5. avoid representing the reconstructed values as newly generated experimental data.

No missing formulations were imputed and no unreported viability values were inferred.
